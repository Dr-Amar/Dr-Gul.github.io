## About AIMEX Lab
AIMEX Lab is a research and innovation initiative founded and directed by **Dr. Muhammad Amar Gul**, advancing AI for mineral exploration through interpretable ML, geochemical big-data analytics, and reproducible scientific workflows.

## Mission
Apply transparent ML to deep-Earth datasets to improve prediction and understanding of mineral deposits.

## Vision
Build reproducible AI systems accelerating mineral exploration with open science and community tools.
